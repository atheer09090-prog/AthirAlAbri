About Me

I'm a Computer Science graduate specialising in Cybersecurity and Network Infrastructure from Sultan Qaboos University, Oman — with hands-on training in penetration testing, network security, digital forensics, and incident response.

My final-year project SmishGuard achieved 99.5% accuracy in detecting SMS phishing attacks using a fine-tuned DistilBERT model with explainable AI (LIME) and VirusTotal integration — bridging technical security and real user awareness.

Outside my core field, I built Bayyin (بيّن) — an Arabic-first interactive science platform for Omani students — as a personal project, driven by a belief that good technology should be accessible and culturally grounded.
