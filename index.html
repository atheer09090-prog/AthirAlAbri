About

Building things that matter.

I'm a Computer Science graduate specialising in Cybersecurity and Network Infrastructure from Sultan Qaboos University, Oman. My work sits at an interesting intersection: keeping systems secure on one side, and building interactive tools that help students learn on the other.

I built Bayyin (بيّن) — a full Arabic interactive science simulation platform aligned to the Omani national curriculum — entirely as a side project. I care about creating technology that is culturally grounded and genuinely useful.
